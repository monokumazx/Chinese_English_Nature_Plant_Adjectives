描摹植物与自然的形容词

Adjectives for Describing Plants & Nature

A bilingual vocabulary guide for learners of all ages. Each entry includes pinyin, definitions in Chinese and English, and a word deconstruction that breaks characters apart to reveal their meaning.

强壮 🆚 弱小

Strong vs. Weak — Is the plant thriving with energy, or is it struggling?

繁茂 ⭐ START fánmào lush; luxuriant; dense and thriving
中文释义(植物) 长得多、长得好、很旺盛的样子。
English Meaning(Of plants) Growing abundantly, healthily, and vigorously.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 繁 + 茂 = 繁茂
繁 (fán) = numerous; abundant; complex  |  茂 (mào) = lush; flourishing; thick
→ So abundant it's flourishing.
茂盛 màoshèng flourishing; exuberant
中文释义植物长得很兴旺、很丰满的样子。
English MeaningPlants growing in a thriving, full state.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 茂 + 盛 = 茂盛
茂 (mào) = lush; flourishing  |  盛 (shèng) = abundant; vigorous; grand
→ Flourishing with abundance.
旺盛 wàngshèng vigorous; full of vitality
中文释义生命力强，精神十足的样子。
English MeaningFull of strong life force and energy.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 旺 + 盛 = 旺盛
旺 (wàng) = vigorous; prosperous; blazing  |  盛 (shèng) = abundant; vigorous
→ Blazing with vigor.
茁壮 zhuózhuàng sturdy and robust; healthy and strong
中文释义(年轻人、动植物) 长得结实、强壮。
English Meaning(Of young living things) Growing solidly and strongly.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 茁 + 壮 = 茁壮
茁 (zhuó) = sprouting; healthy growth  |  壮 (zhuàng) = strong; robust; sturdy
→ Growing up strong.
生机勃勃 shēngjī bóbó vibrant; full of life
中文释义生命力非常充足，很有活力的样子。
English MeaningOverflowing with life force and vitality.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 生机 + 勃勃 = 生机勃勃
生机 (shēngjī) = vitality; life force  |  勃勃 (bóbó) = exuberant; thriving
→ Vitality thrums and thrives.
枯萎 kūwěi withered; wilted
中文释义植物因为缺水或衰老而变干、变黄、低下头的状态。
English MeaningPlants becoming dry, yellow, and droopy due to lack of water or age.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 枯 + 萎 = 枯萎
枯 (kū) = dried up; withered  |  萎 (wěi) = wilted; drooping
→ Dried up and drooping.
奄奄一息 yǎnyǎn yī xī on its last legs; dying; barely breathing
中文释义呼吸很微弱，只剩最后一口气了，快要死去的状态。
English MeaningBreathing so weakly that only a last breath remains.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 奄奄 + 一 + 息 = 奄奄一息
奄奄 (yǎnyǎn) = gasping; faint  |  一 (yī) = one  |  息 (xī) = breath
→ Gasping with only one breath left.
病恹恹 bìng yānyān sickly-looking; languishing
中文释义看起来像生病了一样，无精打采的。
English MeaningLooking as if sick; listless and lacking energy.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 病 + 恹恹 = 病恹恹
病 (bìng) = sick; illness  |  恹恹 (yānyān) = weak and weary; languid
→ Sick and weary-looking.

茂密 🆚 稀疏

Thick vs. Sparse — Is the foliage tightly packed, or loosely scattered?

茂密 màomì dense; thick (of foliage)
中文释义(树木、叶子) 长得又多又密，很难看透过去。
English Meaning(Of trees, leaves) So many and close together it's hard to see through.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 茂 + 密 = 茂密
茂 (mào) = lush; flourishing  |  密 (mì) = dense; close; tight
→ Lushly dense.
浓密 nóngmì thick and dense
中文释义非常稠密，又厚又密实的感觉。
English MeaningVery dense, with a feeling of thickness and compactness.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 浓 + 密 = 浓密
浓 (nóng) = concentrated; thick  |  密 (mì) = dense; close
→ Concentrated and dense.
密不透风 mì bù tòu fēng so dense that wind cannot pass through
中文释义太密集了，连风都吹不过去。
English MeaningSo dense that even the wind cannot penetrate.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 密 + 不 + 透 + 风 = 密不透风
密 (mì) = dense  |  不 (bù) = not  |  透 (tòu) = to pass through  |  风 (fēng) = wind
→ Dense, wind cannot pass.
稀疏 xīshū sparse; thin; scattered
中文释义东西之间空隙大，不密集。
English MeaningHaving large gaps between things; not dense.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 稀 + 疏 = 稀疏
稀 (xī) = sparse; rare; watery  |  疏 (shū) = sparse; scattered; thin
→ Doubly sparse and scattered.
光秃秃 guāng tūtū bare; bald
中文释义上面什么都没有，光光的。
English MeaningWith nothing on top; completely bare.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 光 + 秃秃 = 光秃秃
光 (guāng) = bare; empty  |  秃秃 (tūtū) = bald; bare
→ Bare and bald.
疏疏落落 shūshū luòluò sparse and scattered; few and far between
中文释义稀稀拉拉的，一个一个分散得很开。
English MeaningWidely scattered, one here and one there.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 疏疏 + 落落 = 疏疏落落
疏疏 (shūshū) = sparse  |  落落 (luòluò) = scattered; falling here and there
→ Sparsely scattered about.

色彩

Color & Brightness — Vivid and glossy, or dull and faded?

翠绿 cuìlǜ emerald green; vivid jade-green
中文释义像翡翠宝石一样的亮绿色，很鲜艳。
English MeaningA bright green like jade gemstones; very vivid.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 翠 + 绿 = 翠绿
翠 (cuì) = jade; emerald; vivid blue-green  |  绿 (lǜ) = green
→ Jade green.
葱郁 cōngyù verdant; lush and green
中文释义(山坡、树林) 绿色很浓，长得很茂盛的样子。
English Meaning(Of hillsides, forests) Deeply green and growing lushly.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 葱 + 郁 = 葱郁
葱 (cōng) = deep green of scallions  |  郁 (yù) = lush; dense
→ Scallion-green and lush.
嫩绿 nènlǜ tender light green; new green
中文释义像刚长出来的小芽一样，淡淡的、软软的绿色。
English MeaningA light, soft green like a newly sprouted bud.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 嫩 + 绿 = 嫩绿
嫩 (nèn) = tender; delicate; young  |  绿 (lǜ) = green
→ Tender young green.
鲜艳 xiānyàn bright and colorful; vivid
中文释义(颜色) 又新鲜又明亮，很引人注目。
English Meaning(Of color) Fresh and bright; eye-catching.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 鲜 + 艳 = 鲜艳
鲜 (xiān) = fresh; bright  |  艳 (yàn) = gorgeous; colorful
→ Fresh and gorgeous.
油亮 yóuliàng glossy; shiny (as if oiled)
中文释义表面有光泽，亮亮的，好像涂了一层薄薄的油。
English MeaningHaving a shiny surface, as if polished with oil.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 油 + 亮 = 油亮
油 (yóu) = oil; grease  |  亮 (liàng) = bright; shiny
→ Oil-shiny.
枯黄 kūhuáng withered and yellow; brownish-yellow
中文释义植物干枯后变成的黄褐色。
English MeaningThe yellowish-brown plants turn when dried and withered.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 枯 + 黄 = 枯黄
枯 (kū) = dried up; withered  |  黄 (huáng) = yellow
→ Withered yellow.
黯淡 àndàn dull; dim; lackluster
中文释义没有光泽，颜色不亮，看起来灰灰的。
English MeaningLacking luster; colors not bright, looking grayish.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 黯 + 淡 = 黯淡
黯 (àn) = gloomy; dim; dark  |  淡 (dàn) = light; diluted; dull
→ Gloomy and dull.

形状与姿态

Shape & Pose — Standing straight and tall, or bending and twisting?

挺拔 tǐngbá tall and straight; erect
中文释义站得很直，不弯曲，显得很有精神。
English MeaningStanding very straight, unbent, looking spirited.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 挺 + 拔 = 挺拔
挺 (tǐng) = to stand straight  |  拔 (bá) = to pull up; to stand tall
→ Standing tall as if pulled upward.
笔直 bǐzhí perfectly straight; ramrod-straight
中文释义像笔一样直，一点也不弯。
English MeaningAs straight as a pen; not bent at all.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 笔 + 直 = 笔直
笔 (bǐ) = pen; writing brush  |  直 (zhí) = straight
→ Pen-straight.
粗壮 cūzhuàng thick and stout; burly
中文释义(树干、身体) 又粗又结实，很强壮。
English Meaning(Of a trunk, body) Thick and solid; very strong.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 粗 + 壮 = 粗壮
粗 (cū) = thick; coarse; wide  |  壮 (zhuàng) = strong; sturdy
→ Thick and strong.
修长 xiūcháng tall and slender; graceful
中文释义细长而优美。
English MeaningSlim, long, and graceful.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 修 + 长 = 修长
修 (xiū) = slender; long  |  长 (cháng) = long; length
→ Slender and long.
婀娜 ēnuó willowy and graceful; sinuous
中文释义(姿态) 柔软优美，随风轻轻摇摆。
English Meaning(Of posture) Soft, graceful, and gently swaying.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 婀 + 娜 = 婀娜
婀 (ē) = graceful; willowy  |  娜 (nuó) = elegant; slender; graceful
→ Doubly graceful and willowy.
垂 chuí drooping; hanging down
中文释义头或枝条向下挂着，低低的。
English MeaningHead or branches hanging downward, lowered.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 垂 = 垂
垂 (chuí) = to hang down; to droop; to dangle
→ A single character meaning to hang or droop.
盘曲 pánqū twisting and coiling; gnarled
中文释义弯弯绕绕地卷在一起。
English MeaningWinding and curling around together.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 盘 + 曲 = 盘曲
盘 (pán) = to coil; to wind  |  曲 (qū) = bent; curved; crooked
→ Coiled and curved.
歪歪扭扭 wāiwāi niǔniǔ crooked; twisted; all askew
中文释义长得不正，歪来歪去，扭来扭去的。
English MeaningGrowing off-center; bending and twisting all around.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 歪歪 + 扭扭 = 歪歪扭扭
歪歪 (wāiwāi) = crooked; askew  |  扭扭 (niǔniǔ) = twisted; wrenched
→ Crooked and twisted all over.

触感

Touch & Texture — Smooth and soft, or rough and prickly?

光滑 guānghuá smooth
中文释义表面很平很滑，摸起来很舒服。
English MeaningSurface is flat and slippery; pleasant to touch.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 光 + 滑 = 光滑
光 (guāng) = smooth; glossy  |  滑 (huá) = slippery; smooth
→ Glossy and smooth.
柔嫩 róunèn soft and tender
中文释义又软又娇嫩，好像轻轻一碰就会受伤。
English MeaningSoft and delicately tender, as if a light touch would harm it.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 柔 + 嫩 = 柔嫩
柔 (róu) = soft; flexible; gentle  |  嫩 (nèn) = tender; delicate
→ Softly tender.
毛茸茸 máo rōngrōng fuzzy; downy; furry
中文释义表面上有一层细小的毛，摸起来软软绒绒的。
English MeaningHaving a layer of fine hair; soft and fuzzy to touch.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 毛 + 茸茸 = 毛茸茸
毛 (máo) = hair; fur  |  茸茸 (rōngrōng) = fluffy; downy
→ Hairy and downy-fluffy.
厚实 hòushi thick and sturdy
中文释义又厚又结实，不薄。
English MeaningThick and solid; not thin.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 厚 + 实 = 厚实
厚 (hòu) = thick; deep  |  实 (shi) = solid; substantial
→ Thick and substantial.
粗糙 cūcāo rough; coarse
中文释义表面不光滑，摸起来剌手。
English MeaningSurface is not smooth; feels scratchy.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 粗 + 糙 = 粗糙
粗 (cū) = coarse; rough  |  糙 (cāo) = rough; unpolished
→ Coarsely rough.
多刺 duō cì prickly; thorny
中文释义长了很多尖尖的刺。
English MeaningGrowing many sharp thorns/spines.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 多 + 刺 = 多刺
多 (duō) = many; much  |  刺 (cì) = thorn; spike; to stab
→ Many thorns.

好词连用

Compound Phrases — Expressions that combine multiple ideas into one vivid picture.

枝繁叶茂 zhī fán yè mào with lush branches and dense leaves
中文释义树枝很多，叶子很密，形容树木长得很旺盛。
English MeaningBranches abundant and leaves dense; a tree growing vigorously.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 枝 + 繁 + 叶 + 茂 = 枝繁叶茂
枝 (zhī) = branch  |  繁 (fán) = abundant  |  叶 (yè) = leaf  |  茂 (mào) = lush
→ Branches abundant, leaves lush.
根深叶茂 gēn shēn yè mào deep roots and lush leaves
中文释义根扎得深，叶子才茂盛。比喻基础扎实。
English MeaningRoots are deep, so leaves are lush; a solid foundation.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 根 + 深 + 叶 + 茂 = 根深叶茂
根 (gēn) = root  |  深 (shēn) = deep  |  叶 (yè) = leaf  |  茂 (mào) = lush
→ Roots deep, leaves lush.
亭亭玉立 tíngtíng yùlì slender and graceful
中文释义身材细长而优美地站着，像玉石雕刻一样。
English MeaningStanding tall, slender, and graceful, like a jade carving.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 亭亭 + 玉 + 立 = 亭亭玉立
亭亭 (tíngtíng) = tall and erect  |  玉 (yù) = jade  |  立 (lì) = to stand
→ Standing gracefully like jade.
绿意盎然 lǜ yì àng rán bursting with green vitality
中文释义绿色的生机充满了，到处都是活力的绿。
English MeaningGreen life force overflowing; vibrant green everywhere.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 绿意 + 盎然 = 绿意盎然
绿意 (lǜ yì) = green vitality  |  盎然 (àng rán) = abundant; overflowing
→ Green vitality overflowing.
弱不禁风 ruò bù jīn fēng so fragile a breeze could break it
中文释义弱得连轻微的风都承受不住，非常娇弱。
English MeaningSo weak it cannot withstand a light breeze; extremely delicate.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 弱 + 不 + 禁 + 风 = 弱不禁风
弱 (ruò) = weak  |  不 (bù) = not  |  禁 (jīn) = to withstand  |  风 (fēng) = wind
→ Too weak to bear the wind.

天气与气候

Weather & Climate — Describing the sky, air, and conditions above.

晴朗 qínglǎng sunny and clear; bright
中文释义天空没有云，阳光明亮。
English MeaningCloudless sky with bright sunshine.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 晴 + 朗 = 晴朗
晴 (qíng) = clear (sky); fine weather  |  朗 (lǎng) = bright; luminous
→ Clear and bright.
阴沉 yīnchén overcast; gloomy; somber
中文释义天色很暗，云层很厚，看起来像是要下雨。
English MeaningDark sky with thick clouds, looking like it might rain.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 阴 + 沉 = 阴沉
阴 (yīn) = overcast; cloudy; dark  |  沉 (chén) = heavy; sinking; deep
→ Dark and heavy.
潮湿 cháoshī humid; damp; moist
中文释义空气中水气很多，东西摸起来润润的。
English MeaningAir contains a lot of moisture; things feel damp.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 潮 + 湿 = 潮湿
潮 (cháo) = moist; tide; damp  |  湿 (shī) = wet; damp
→ Moist and damp.
干旱 gānhàn arid; dry; drought-stricken
中文释义很久没下雨，土地都干裂了。
English MeaningLong without rain; land is dry and cracked.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 干 + 旱 = 干旱
干 (gān) = dry  |  旱 (hàn) = drought; dry spell
→ Dry and drought-ridden.
温和 wēnhé mild; temperate; gentle
中文释义不冷不热，很舒服。
English MeaningNeither hot nor cold; very comfortable.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 温 + 和 = 温和
温 (wēn) = warm; mild  |  和 (hé) = gentle; harmonious
→ Warm and gentle.
凛冽 lǐnliè bitterly cold; piercing
中文释义(寒风) 非常冷，像刀割一样。
English Meaning(Of cold wind) So cold it cuts like a knife.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 凛 + 冽 = 凛冽
凛 (lǐn) = freezing; stern; severe  |  冽 (liè) = icy cold; piercing
→ Freezing and piercing cold.

地貌与风景

Landscape & Scenery — Describing the land beneath our feet.

肥沃 féiwò fertile; rich (soil)
中文释义(土地) 养分很多，适合植物生长。
English Meaning(Of land) Rich in nutrients; good for growing plants.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 肥 + 沃 = 肥沃
肥 (féi) = fat; fertile; rich  |  沃 (wò) = fertile; rich; irrigated
→ Rich and fertile.
贫瘠 pínjí barren; infertile; poor (soil)
中文释义(土地) 没有营养，植物很难生长。
English Meaning(Of land) Lacking nutrients; hard for plants to grow.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 贫 + 瘠 = 贫瘠
贫 (pín) = poor; impoverished  |  瘠 (jí) = barren; thin; lean
→ Poor and barren.
辽阔 liáokuò vast; expansive; wide
中文释义范围非常大，一眼望不到边。
English MeaningSo large in scope you cannot see the edge.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 辽 + 阔 = 辽阔
辽 (liáo) = distant; far; vast  |  阔 (kuò) = wide; broad; expansive
→ Distant and wide.
崎岖 qíqū rugged; uneven; rough (terrain)
中文释义(山路) 不平坦，高高低低，很难走。
English Meaning(Of mountain paths) Uneven and bumpy; hard to walk.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 崎 + 岖 = 崎岖
崎 (qí) = rugged; uneven  |  岖 (qū) = precipitous; rugged
→ Rugged and precipitous.
荒芜 huāngwú desolate; barren; overgrown with weeds
中文释义土地没人打理，长满了野草，很荒凉。
English MeaningLand untended, overgrown with weeds, desolate.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 荒 + 芜 = 荒芜
荒 (huāng) = desolate; barren; wild  |  芜 (wú) = overgrown with weeds; messy
→ Desolate and overgrown.
草甸 cǎodiàn meadow; grassy plain
中文释义长满野草和野花的低矮平地，多出现在山区或河边。
English MeaningA flat lowland area covered with wild grass and flowers, often found in mountains or by rivers.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 草 + 甸 = 草甸
草 (cǎo) = grass; plants  |  甸 (diàn) = open land; pasture; flat countryside
→ A grassy open land — a meadow.
📝 Example 夏天的草甸上开满了五颜六色的野花。 In summer, the meadow is covered with colorful wildflowers.
静谧 jìngmì quiet; tranquil; serene
中文释义非常安静，没有吵闹的声音，让人安心。
English MeaningVery quiet, without noise; gives a sense of peace.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 静 + 谧 = 静谧
静 (jìng) = quiet; still; calm  |  谧 (mì) = serene; peaceful; quiet
→ Quiet and serene.

自然元素

Natural Elements — Water, light, air, and the sensory qualities of nature.

清澈 qīngchè crystal clear; limpid
中文释义(水) 非常干净透明，可以看到底。
English Meaning(Of water) So clean and transparent you can see the bottom.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 清 + 澈 = 清澈
清 (qīng) = clear; pure; clean  |  澈 (chè) = limpid; clear; transparent
→ Clear and transparent.
浑浊 húnzhuó murky; turbid; muddy
中文释义(水、空气) 不干净，有很多杂质，看不透。
English Meaning(Of water, air) Not clean; full of particles; cannot see through.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 浑 + 浊 = 浑浊
浑 (hún) = muddy; turbid; mixed-up  |  浊 (zhuó) = muddy; turbid; impure
→ Muddy and impure.
芬芳 fēnfāng fragrant; sweet-smelling
中文释义(花、草) 闻起来香香的，很好闻。
English Meaning(Of flowers, grass) Smelling sweet and pleasant.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 芬 + 芳 = 芬芳
芬 (fēn) = fragrance; sweet smell  |  芳 (fāng) = fragrant; aromatic
→ Sweet-smelling and aromatic.
刺鼻 cìbí pungent; sharp-smelling; irritating to the nose
中文释义气味很重，让鼻子不舒服。
English MeaningA strong smell that irritates the nose.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 刺 + 鼻 = 刺鼻
刺 (cì) = to stab; to irritate  |  鼻 (bí) = nose
→ Stabs the nose.
明媚 míngmèi bright and beautiful; radiant
中文释义(阳光、景色) 又明亮又好看，让人心情愉快。
English Meaning(Of sunlight, scenery) Bright and beautiful; lifts the mood.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 明 + 媚 = 明媚
明 (míng) = bright; clear  |  媚 (mèi) = charming; beautiful; enchanting
→ Bright and charming.
柔和 róuhé soft; gentle; mild
中文释义(光线、声音、风) 轻轻的，不强烈，很舒服。
English Meaning(Of light, sound, wind) Light, not intense, very comfortable.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 柔 + 和 = 柔和
柔 (róu) = soft; gentle  |  和 (hé) = gentle; harmonious
→ Soft and gentle.
